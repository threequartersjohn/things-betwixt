Things Betwixt

And people between.

I often take photos of places or things pass by quickly and can be very small but that i like nonetheless. I am very fond of them precisely because they bring focus to what can otherwise be easy to miss.

While these photos are still appropriate for sharing without context in social media, i think they are still deserving of recognition and organization.

This project tries to give these photos meaning by adding context and linearity.

That's it for now. Feel free go back and look at the photos more carefully.

Thank you.
